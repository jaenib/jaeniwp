Overview

Invisibility

Cartographic and cinematic studies on what cities hide in plain sight.

Invisibility assembles heat maps, spatial scans, and cinematic fragments to reveal how public life concentrates, disperses, and disappears. The work treats visibility as a political layer, not just an optical one.

Signals

Maps and traces

  • Heat maps tracking cultural intensity and quiet zones.
  • Orthographic drawings that stack time-based activity.
  • Atmospheric renders to expose hidden thresholds.
